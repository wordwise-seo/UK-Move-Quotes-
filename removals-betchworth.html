Removals in Betchworth

Betchworth is a picturesque Surrey village on the A25 between Reigate and Dorking, sitting at the foot of the North Downs on the north bank of the River Mole. The village has a strong concentration of period and character properties, including many listed buildings within the Betchworth Conservation Area, and the ruins of Betchworth Castle sit just outside the village near neighbouring Brockham.

The area covers both Betchworth and Brockham, two closely linked villages that share a strong sense of community and a similarly high proportion of period cottages and larger detached homes. Properties here command some of the highest average prices in the wider RH postcode area, so removal companies covering Betchworth are often used to handling higher value and more delicate moves, including antiques, art, and larger period furniture that needs careful handling through older doorways and staircases.
