Removals in Godstone

Godstone is a classic Surrey village built around Godstone Green and its duck pond, with historic buildings dating back to the 15th century framing the open space. The village splits into two distinct areas, the Green itself with its cluster of period cottages and pubs, and South Godstone around two miles south, which has its own railway station and a different, more modern character.

The village sits at the junction of the A22 and A25, close to the M25 at junction 6, which makes it well connected despite its rural feel. This means removal companies covering Godstone are generally comfortable with both the tight, older streets around the Green and the more standard suburban roads around South Godstone.
