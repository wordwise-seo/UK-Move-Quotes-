Tips for getting the most from your man and van in Crawley

Same day is often possible

Many man with a van operators in Crawley can do same day or next day jobs when their schedule allows. Mention in your request that you need it quickly and operators who can help will respond. Crawley town centre and Three Bridges have operators available at short notice most days.

Mention the tricky bits upfront

Narrow staircase in a Crawley town centre flat, no parking on your street in Ifield, a wardrobe that barely came through the door. Tell the operator everything upfront. It helps them come prepared with the right equipment and means no surprises on the day that could affect your quote.

Have everything ready when they arrive

The fastest and cheapest man and van jobs are where everything is boxed, bagged, and stacked near the door when the driver arrives. Every minute spent waiting for you to finish packing is time on the clock if you are paying hourly.

Ask about insurance

Reputable man with a van operators carry goods in transit insurance as standard. It is worth asking specifically when you book and confirming what is covered. Valuable or fragile items may need additional cover arranged separately.

Fixed price beats hourly for short moves

For a straightforward local move within Crawley, ask for a fixed price rather than an hourly rate. A fixed price protects you if loading takes longer than expected and means you know exactly what you are paying before the van arrives.

Two person teams are often worth it

A second person makes loading and carrying significantly faster, particularly for heavy furniture. For a one bedroom flat it can halve the time on an hourly job and more than pay for itself. Ask when requesting your quote whether a two person team is available.
