What affects the cost of your move?

The two biggest factors are the size of your property and the distance you are moving. A two-bedroom flat moving to a street nearby is a very different job to a four-bedroom house heading to the other side of the county. The time of year and your move date also play a part. Fridays and the end of the month are the busiest days for removal companies in Crawley, which can affect availability and sometimes price.

Common questions

Things people ask us about house removals in Crawley

How far in advance should I book a removal company?

For most moves in Crawley, four to six weeks is comfortable. If you are moving at the end of the month or on a Friday in summer, try to book earlier. Those slots fill quickly and the companies with the best reputations get taken first.

Do I need to be at the property on move day?

Yes. Someone needs to be present to let the crew in, point out anything that needs special care, and check everything is loaded correctly. If you cannot be there the whole time, arrange for someone you trust to be present in your place.

Are my belongings insured during the move?

Reputable removal companies carry goods-in-transit insurance as standard. It is worth asking each company about the level of cover before you book, particularly if you have valuable or fragile items.

What if my completion date changes?

It happens more than you might think. Most removal companies in Crawley are used to dealing with last-minute changes tied to the conveyancing process. Ask about their rescheduling policy when you book so there are no surprises if things shift.
