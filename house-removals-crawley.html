What affects the cost of your move?

The two biggest factors are property size and distance. A two-bedroom flat moving to a nearby street is a very different job to a four-bedroom house heading across the county. The time of year and your move date also play a part. Fridays and the end of the month are the busiest days for removal companies in Crawley, which can affect availability and sometimes price.

Common questions

Things people ask about house removals in Crawley

How far in advance should I book?

Four to six weeks is comfortable for most moves in Crawley. If you are moving at the end of the month or on a Friday in summer, try to book earlier. The best companies get taken first.

Do I need to be at the property on move day?

Yes. Someone needs to be present to let the crew in, point out anything needing special care, and check everything is loaded correctly. If you cannot be there, arrange for someone you trust.

Are my belongings insured during the move?

Reputable removal companies carry goods-in-transit insurance as standard. Ask each company about the level of cover before you book, particularly if you have valuable or fragile items.

What if my completion date changes?

It happens more than you might think. Most removal companies in Crawley are used to last-minute changes tied to conveyancing. Ask about their rescheduling policy when you book.
