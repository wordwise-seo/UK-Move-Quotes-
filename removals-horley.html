Removals in Horley

Horley sits right on the Surrey and West Sussex border next to Gatwick Airport, giving it a genuinely mixed character, a historic town centre alongside significant new housing growth over recent years. The town has several distinct residential areas beyond the centre itself, including Acres, a well established neighbourhood of family homes, and Gardens Estate near Riverside Garden Park.

Westvale Park, to the north west of the town joining onto Meath Green, is one of the largest new build developments in the area, with around 1,600 new homes built by a consortium of housebuilders including Taylor Wimpey and Crest Nicholson, alongside a new primary school and planned community facilities. This kind of large scale new development means removal companies covering Horley regularly handle a mix of moves, from established family homes to brand new builds still being finished around them.
