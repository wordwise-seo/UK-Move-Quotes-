Removals across Crawley and the Gatwick corridor.

Moving to or from the Gatwick area

The Gatwick area covers a cluster of towns and villages immediately surrounding the airport including Horley, Charlwood, Smallfield, Salfords, and parts of Redhill. It is one of the most active areas for house moves in Surrey and West Sussex, with a constantly changing population connected to the aviation industry and the wider commuter belt.

Moving between Crawley and the Gatwick corridor is a short hop. Most of these moves are under ten miles and take a matter of hours for most property sizes. Local removal companies in Crawley cover this area as a matter of course.

The Gatwick area has good road access which makes removals relatively straightforward. Most residential streets around Horley and Charlwood have reasonable parking and the properties tend to be houses rather than flats, which removes many of the access complications that can slow a move down.

Gatwick to London removals

If you are moving from the Gatwick area up to London, or from London down to Gatwick, this is also a well travelled route for removal companies based in Crawley. The M23 and A23 give good access in both directions and most experienced companies plan around the traffic patterns they know well on this corridor.
