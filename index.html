Common questions

Things people ask before they get quotes

Is it really free to use?

Yes, completely. Getting quotes through UK Move Quotes costs you nothing. We work with the removal companies, who pay us when we send them a lead. You never pay us a penny at any point.

How quickly will removal companies get in touch?

Most people hear back within a few hours. We pass your details to removal companies immediately after you submit the form. During busy periods or if you submit late in the evening you may hear back the following morning instead.

How many companies will contact me?

Typically between one and three local removal companies. We do not flood your inbox with calls from dozens of companies. The idea is to give you a realistic comparison, not to overwhelm you.

Do I have to commit to anything by requesting quotes?

Not at all. Requesting quotes is completely non-committal. You are under no obligation to book with any company that contacts you. If none of the quotes work for you, that is fine.

What areas do you cover?

We currently cover Crawley and the surrounding towns in West Sussex and Surrey including Horley, Horsham, East Grinstead, Redhill, Reigate, Burgess Hill, and Haywards Heath. We are adding more locations regularly.

What if I do not know my move date yet?

That is completely fine. Select the not decided yet option on the form. Removal companies are used to dealing with enquiries at an early stage and can still give you a rough idea of pricing based on your property size and postcodes.
