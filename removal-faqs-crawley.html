Removal FAQs — your questions answered

The most common questions about removal costs, removal companies, and house moves in Crawley, West Sussex, and Surrey.

Removal costs in Crawley

What is the average cost of a removal company in Crawley for a 3 bedroom house?

A three bedroom house removal in Crawley typically costs between £550 and £950 for a local move within the RH10 and RH11 postcode areas. Longer distance moves — to London, Brighton, or further afield — will cost more depending on distance and access at both properties. Getting quotes from two or three local companies is the best way to see what your specific job comes to. See our full Crawley removal costs guide for a breakdown by property size.

How do removal companies in Crawley calculate their pricing?

Removal companies price based on several factors: property size and the estimated volume of furniture and boxes, the distance between the two properties, access at both ends (stairs, parking, narrow roads), whether a packing service is included, and the day of the week. Friday and end of month moves are typically more expensive because of higher demand. Weekend moves are also busier and often priced accordingly.

Are removal costs in Crawley cheaper on weekdays than weekends?

Yes, in general. Midweek moves from Tuesday to Thursday are typically the most affordable because demand is lower. Friday is consistently the most expensive day for removals in Crawley — most property completions happen on Fridays and removal companies know this. Saturday moves are busier than midweek but availability is more flexible than Friday. If you have any flexibility in your move date, Tuesday or Wednesday tends to give you the best combination of price and availability.

How can I get an accurate removal quote in Crawley without any hidden fees?

Ask for a written fixed price quote rather than an hourly rate estimate. Mention any complicating factors upfront — stairs with no lift, parking restrictions, large or heavy items like pianos or gym equipment, or access limitations at either property. The more detail you give the more accurate the quote. A reputable company will give you a fixed price that covers everything discussed. If a company quotes hourly, ask for an estimated total and confirm what happens if the job runs over time.

What factors affect the cost of house removals in Crawley?

The main factors are property size and volume of belongings, distance between properties, access at both ends (stairs, parking, road width), the day of the week, time of year (summer is more expensive), whether packing is included, and any specialist items. Moving in or out of a flat with no lift adds significantly more time and cost than a ground floor property with good road access.

Booking and timing

How far in advance should I book a removal company in Crawley?

Four to six weeks ahead is comfortable for most moves. If you are moving on a Friday, at the end of the month, or during summer (June to September) book as early as possible — good local companies in Crawley fill up weeks in advance during busy periods. If you have an urgent move with less than two weeks notice, mention this when you request quotes and companies who can accommodate short notice will respond.

What is the best time of year to move house in Crawley?

Autumn and winter — September to February — are generally the quietest and most affordable times to move in Crawley and West Sussex. Summer is the busiest period, particularly July and August. Spring (March to May) is mid-season with good availability and reasonable prices. Read our full guide to the best time to move in West Sussex for more detail.

Should I pay a deposit to book a removal company in Crawley?

Most reputable removal companies ask for a deposit of ten to twenty five percent when you book. This is standard practice and holds your date. Be cautious about companies asking for fifty percent or more upfront. Always get a receipt and make sure the cancellation and postponement policy is clear in writing before you pay anything.
