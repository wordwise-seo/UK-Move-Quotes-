Who we are

We make finding a removal company in Crawley straightforward.

Moving home involves enough stress without spending hours searching the internet, reading through dozens of websites, ringing round companies one at a time, and trying to work out who is actually good and who just has a well-designed website.

UK Move Quotes was built to solve that. You fill in one short form telling us about your move, and we pass your details to removal companies in and around Crawley who are ready to quote. Why we focus on local companies

There is a reason people consistently rate local removal companies higher than national operators. A Crawley-based company has done this route dozens of times. They know the parking situation on Manor Royal. They know which roads get clogged up on a Friday afternoon. They have relationships with local solicitors and they are used to working around the unpredictability that comes with conveyancing completions.

National companies send whoever is available. Local companies send people who work in the area every week. For most people that makes a genuine difference to how their move day actually feels.

How it works

When you submit your details through our form, your enquiry is passed to removal companies covering your area. They receive your contact information, your move details, and they get in touch directly to discuss your requirements and give you a quote.

We do not take a cut of what you pay the removal company. We do not sit in the middle of the conversation. Once the introduction is made, the arrangement is entirely between you and the company you choose to go with.

What we ask of the companies we work with

We are selective about the removal companies we pass leads to. We look for companies that are established, have good reviews, carry proper insurance, and respond to enquiries promptly. The last thing we want is someone submitting their details and then hearing nothing back.

If you ever have a problem with a company you were connected with through UK Move Quotes, we want to hear about it. We take the quality of our network seriously and feedback helps us keep it that way.
