Price guide, 2025

How much do removals cost in Crawley?

Removal costs in Crawley vary depending on the size of your home, how far you are moving, and when you need to go. This guide gives you realistic price ranges so you know what to expect before you start getting quotes.

Typical removal costs in Crawley by property size

Property size is the biggest single driver of removal costs. These figures are for local moves within West Sussex or into neighbouring Surrey and should be treated as a guide rather than a fixed price.

Property sizeTypical range
Studio or 1 bed flat£250 to £500
2 bed property£400 to £750
3 bed house£600 to £1,100
4 bed house£900 to £1,500
5 bed or larger£1,400 upwards
Man with a van (hourly)£50 to £90/hr

What pushes the price up

Access is the thing that catches people out most. A third floor flat with no lift and street parking 80 metres away is a much harder job than a ground floor house with a driveway. Removal companies factor in the time it takes to carry items to and from the van, and difficult access genuinely adds time to a job.

The time of year matters too. Late spring and summer are the busiest months for removals in West Sussex. If you can move between October and March you will generally find more availability and often more flexibility on price.

End of month Fridays are consistently the most in-demand days throughout the year. If you have flexibility in your moving date even by a day or two it can open up more options.

How to keep costs down

Getting at least three quotes is the single most effective thing you can do. Prices for the same job can vary by a few hundred pounds between companies. Decluttering before you move reduces volume. Packing your own boxes saves the time a crew would otherwise spend on packing. Being flexible on your date gives you more choices.

Good to know

Questions about removal costs in Crawley

Should I pay a deposit to secure my removal company?

Most reputable removal companies in Crawley will ask for a deposit when you book, typically between 10 and 25 percent of the total price. Make sure you get written confirmation of the booking and understand the cancellation terms before you pay anything.

Are the prices I see online always accurate?

Prices quoted online are based on averages and should be treated as a guide. The actual price you are quoted depends on the specifics of your move, which is why getting real quotes from companies who have seen your details is always more useful than any price guide.

Do removal companies charge more for stairs or difficult access?

They can do, yes. Being upfront about access challenges when you request quotes means you get an accurate price the first time rather than a surprise on the day.
