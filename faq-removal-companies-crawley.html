Frequently asked questions

Everything people ask about removal companies in Crawley.

Answers to the most common questions about removal costs, booking, insurance, and moving house in Crawley and West Sussex.

What is the average cost of a removal company in Crawley for a 3 bedroom house?

A 3 bedroom house removal in Crawley typically costs between £550 and £950 for a local move within West Sussex. Longer distance moves cost more — a move to London for example would typically range from £900 to £1,600. Prices vary depending on access at both properties, distance, time of year, and whether packing is included. The best way to get an accurate price is to get free quotes from local companies who will price your specific move.

How do removal companies in Crawley calculate their pricing?

Removal companies price based on several factors: the size of your property and volume of belongings, the distance between properties, the time required to complete the job, the number of crew members needed, vehicle size, access complications at either end, and the time of year. Fridays and end of month dates are the busiest and often the most expensive. Midweek moves in winter tend to be the cheapest.

Are removal costs in Crawley cheaper on weekdays than weekends?

Yes, generally. Weekday moves — particularly Monday to Thursday — are often cheaper than Friday or weekend moves because removal companies have more availability midweek. The Friday completion rush means removal companies can be fully booked weeks in advance on popular dates. If you have flexibility with your moving date, choosing midweek can save money and gives you more choice of company.

How can I get an accurate removal quote in Crawley without hidden fees?

Give removal companies your full move details — both postcodes, exact property sizes, any access complications at either property, your move date, and whether you need packing materials or packing services. Always ask for a fixed price quote rather than an estimate. A fixed price protects you if the job takes longer than expected. Get everything in writing before you commit and ask specifically whether there are any additional charges that could apply.

How many removal companies should I get quotes from?

Two to three quotes is usually enough to get a fair picture of the market price for your move. Prices for identical jobs can vary significantly between companies so comparing is always worth doing. UK Move Quotes sends your details to local companies simultaneously so you can compare without ringing round yourself.

What is included in a removal quote?

A standard removal quote covers loading your belongings at the old property, transporting them to the new property, and unloading at the other end. Packing materials, packing services, dismantling and reassembling furniture, and storage are usually extra unless specifically included. Always check what is covered before accepting a quote to avoid surprises.

How far in advance should I book a removal company in Crawley?

Four to six weeks ahead is comfortable for most moves. If you are moving at the end of the month, on a Friday, or during the summer peak between May and September, book as early as possible. Good removal companies in Crawley fill up fast on popular dates. Some companies can accommodate moves at shorter notice but availability becomes limited on the dates most people want.

Do removal companies in Crawley cover long distance moves?

Yes, most established removal companies in Crawley cover long distance moves across the UK. Common routes include Crawley to London, Crawley to Brighton, and moves to other major cities. Some companies also cover European and international moves. Mention your destination in the notes field when requesting quotes and companies who cover that route will respond.

What happens if something gets damaged during my move?

Reputable removal companies carry goods in transit insurance. If something gets damaged report it to the company immediately — ideally on move day — and follow their claims process. Before booking always ask what goods in transit insurance is included, what the maximum claim value is per item, and what the excess is. Get the insurance details in writing as part of your booking confirmation.

Do I need to be at home when the removal company arrives?

Yes, you or a trusted representative should be present at both the loading and delivery addresses on move day. You will need to direct the crew, confirm what is being moved, check the condition of items as they are loaded, and sign off on delivery at the other end. Having someone present protects you if any dispute arises about damage or missing items.

Can removal companies help with packing?

Yes, most removal companies offer a full packing service as an optional extra. They bring all the materials — boxes, tape, bubble wrap, wardrobe boxes — and pack everything for you either the day before the move or on move day itself. This adds to the cost but saves significant time and reduces the risk of damage from poorly packed items. Mention packing in the notes field when requesting quotes.
