Common questions

Things removal companies ask us

How many companies receive each lead?
A maximum of two companies ever receive the same lead. Never more. That means you are never one of five or six companies racing to be heard first.
How quickly do I receive leads after signing up?
We are growing our network across Surrey, West Sussex, East Sussex and Kent, so leads come through as traffic to the site grows in your area. The first five are free however long they take.
What areas do the leads cover?
51 postcode territories across Surrey, West Sussex, East Sussex and Kent, including Crawley, Horsham, Guildford, Brighton, Tunbridge Wells, Maidstone and the surrounding areas. We are expanding regularly.
What about larger jobs — 4 or 5 bed houses?
Larger or higher value moves are priced separately as priority leads at £25, and only offered to companies who confirm they can take them on. You are never sent a job you cannot fulfil.
How do I pay for leads after the trial?
We invoice weekly or monthly by email. Payment by bank transfer or card via Stripe. No payment is taken without your agreement on each lead.
Can I pause or stop at any time?
Yes. There is no contract and no minimum term. Email us at any time to pause or stop receiving leads with no notice period required.
What happens if a lead has incorrect details?
Let us know within 48 hours and we will review it. Leads with invalid contact details or clearly false information are credited back at no charge.
