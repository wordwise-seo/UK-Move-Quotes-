Packing tips from the removal industry

After 13 years in the removals industry here is what the professionals do differently when packing boxes.

  • Heavy items always go in small boxes. Books, crockery, and tools in small boxes only.
  • Label every box on the side not the top — you will stack them and never see the top label.
  • Fill boxes completely. Half empty boxes collapse when stacked and things get damaged.
  • Use scrunched packing paper to fill gaps — it is gentler on fragile items than newspaper.
  • Pack one room at a time and use room stickers so your removal company knows exactly where everything goes.
  • Order more boxes than you think you need. You can always return unused flat packs.
