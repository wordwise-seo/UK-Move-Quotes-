Moving from Haywards Heath to Brighton

Haywards Heath to Brighton is just 15 miles south on the A273 — one of the shortest inter-town removal routes in West Sussex. The journey typically takes 25 to 40 minutes depending on traffic and can be done very comfortably within a single working day for most property sizes.

This is a popular move for people leaving Haywards Heath for the coast and the lifestyle Brighton offers. The reverse move — from Brighton to Haywards Heath — is equally common as people seek more space and quieter surroundings while keeping easy train access to Brighton and London.

Because the distance is short, prices for this route tend to be towards the lower end of the scale. For smaller properties a man with a van is a particularly cost effective option.

Cost of a Haywards Heath to Brighton removal
